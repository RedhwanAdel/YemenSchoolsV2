Admissions Overview

Welcome to Sunrise Academy's Admissions section! We are delighted you are considering our school for your child's education. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and supportive, ensuring a smooth transition for prospective students and their families.

We look for students who are intellectually curious, possess a strong desire to learn, and demonstrate good character. Our admissions team is here to guide you through every step, from initial inquiry to enrollment.

Application Process

  1. Step 1: Online Inquiry & Information Session
    Submit an online inquiry form to receive our admissions packet and register for an optional information session or open house.
  2. Step 2: Submit Application
    Complete the online application form and upload all required documents (academic transcripts, recommendation letters, birth certificate, etc.).
  3. Step 3: Entrance Assessment & Interview
    Prospective students will undergo an age-appropriate entrance assessment and an interview with an admissions officer.
  4. Step 4: Admission Decision & Enrollment
    Families will be notified of the admission decision. Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment agreement and pay the registration fee to secure your child's place.
